Vale Cofer-Shabica, Ph.D.

I'm a theoretical physical chemist studying reaction dynamics through the geometry of potential energy landscapes. I did my dissertation with Richard Stratt at the Brown University Department of Chemistry. In addition to research and helping others to understand new things, I love to bike, cook, and make music. I care deeply about enacting a scientific community that is explicitly welcoming to all women and people of color and that actively opposes long-standing forces of hostility to them and other marginalized persons in the world.
